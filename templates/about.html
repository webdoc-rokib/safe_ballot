SafeBallot

Secure, private, and easy-to-use online voting built for clubs, societies, and small organizations.

Why choose SafeBallot?

We focus on three core promises: privacy, simplicity, and trust. SafeBallot encrypts individual votes, minimizing exposure of voter intent while producing auditable tallies for transparency.

Privacy-first

AES‑GCM encryption ensures ballots are stored as ciphertext until tallying.

Simple admin tools

Upload eligible voters, manage candidates, and publish results with a few clicks.

Problems we solve

  • Reduce manual ballot counting and the errors that come with it.
  • Protect voter anonymity while allowing verifiable outcomes.
  • Provide a reproducible process for small-scale elections without heavy infrastructure.

Who should use SafeBallot?

Student unions, clubs, non-profit boards, local communities, and any organization seeking an accessible, private voting solution without enterprise complexity.

FAQ

Votes are stored encrypted. The system does not attach voter identifiers to individual ciphertexts; eligibility is enforced separately via `VoterStatus`.

If the AES key is lost or rotated without migration, previously cast votes cannot be decrypted. Key management is critical in production.

SafeBallot is designed for small to medium organizations. Governmental-scale elections require specialized legal, security, and operational controls beyond this demo.
