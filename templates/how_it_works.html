How SafeBallot works

A quick overview of the secure voting flow.

1. Create election

Admins define title, description, start/end times, and candidates.

2. Add voters

Upload eligible voters. Each voter gets a ballot assignment when the election starts.

3. Cast votes

Voters sign in and cast an encrypted ballot. We store only ciphertext tied to the election.

4. Tally

After the election ends, results are decrypted and aggregated. Admins can export CSV.

5. Audit

Activity charts and turnout metrics help review participation and detect anomalies.

Security
  • Encrypted ballots (per-election associated data)
  • Voter eligibility tracking
  • Results released only after end time
  • Admin approval for new accounts
